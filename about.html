About Aval Express

Aval Express is a courier concierge and personal shopping company located in Doral, Florida. The company operates through a freight forwarder to Guayaquil, Ecuador, helping customers connect with safe and reliable shipping services for their packages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sure every Ecuadorian family receives their packages safely and quickly. Aval Express values personalized delivery and customer care as an important part of every service we provide.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create personalized access for people in Ecuador so they can take advantage of discounts and products from the United States. Through our personal shopping service, we want to help Ecuadorian families feel happy, connected, and supported.

Our Story

Aval Express was founded in 2025 by Andy Cevallos with the goal of helping customers send packages and shop from the United States in a secure, simple, and dependable way.
