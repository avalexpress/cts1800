Our Services

Aval Express offers courier concierge and personal shopping services from Doral, Florida to Guayaquil, Ecuador. Our goal is to provide clear, reliable, and transparent options for every customer.

Service 1: 4x4 Courier Shipping

The 4x4 category is designed for customers who want to send packages from the United States to Ecuador. This service helps customers receive their items safely while understanding customs costs and shipping fees.

4x4 Shipping Without Personal Shopping

Package Weight Rate Customs Fee Notes
Less than 5 lb $8 per lb Included Customs expenses are already included in the shipping rate.
More than 5 lb $5.50 per lb $20 customs fee This option may be more affordable in specific cases due to prorated costs.

4x4 Shipping With Personal Shopping

Service Fee Shipping Rate Notes
Personal Shopping 20% of each purchase $8 per lb for packages under 5 lb Customs expenses are included in the $8 per lb rate.
Personal Shopping 20% of each purchase $5.50 per lb for packages over 5 lb A $20 customs fee applies for packages over 5 lb.

Service 2: Personal Shopping

Our personal shopping service helps customers in Ecuador access products, discounts, and online deals from stores in the United States. Aval Express assists with the purchase process and shipping coordination.

The personal shopping fee is 20% of each purchase. Shipping charges are calculated separately depending on the weight of the package and the applicable 4x4 category.

Important Information

Aval Express uses these rates because, in certain cases, prorating the shipping and customs costs can be more affordable for the customer. However, it is important to know that the $8 per lb rate already includes customs expenses for each shipment.

We believe in transparency. There are no hidden fees, and every customer receives clear information before confirming a shipment or personal shopping order.
